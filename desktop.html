About me

My name is Jannis, I'm 16 years old and I'm a developer.

I'm visiting the high school Kantonsschule Alpenquai in the 4. year and with focus on Applied Mathematics and Physic.
I've been programming for over 3 years now and have plenty of experience in Java and fullstack web development.

My first contact with programming was 5 years ago in a course about Scratch and game development. After this course I gained interest and started to visit a few other courses.
In summer of 2016 I got the opportunity to get a private course about the development of Spigot Plugins (extensions to the serverside of the very popular game called Minecraft) in Java.

After all of this I started to realize how I can teach those things myself and improved steadily over time.
Meanwhile me and a friend of mine got interested in how websites are build and learned the HTML basics from an WikiHow article and after that researched on sites like w3schools. I didn't understand CSS for a long time, but then I experienced a moment of enlightenment and suddenly everything made sense. I started to develop my first own website and got in touch with php while trying to make a contact form.

In the last year I did some contract work and some projects on my own. A few of my latest projects were:

  • A little highly configurable wallpaper engine in Java
  • A JSON parser in Java
  • this amazing website
  • my a little bit older website, witch is still available under this link (but watch out, it's a nerdy website you may not be able to navigate around).
